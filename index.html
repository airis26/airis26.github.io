ABOUT THE CONFERENCE

What is AIRIS'26?

The First National Conference on Artificial Intelligence: Research, Innovation & Systems (AIRIS'26) is a premier event organized by the Faculty of Exact Sciences at the University of Mascara in partnership with the LABTEC AI laboratory.

This conference brings together leading researchers, industry experts, and policymakers to explore cutting-edge AI advances and shape the future of intelligent systems in Algeria.

The event features keynote lectures, technical sessions, panel discussions, and networking opportunities focused on emerging AI technologies and their societal impact.
